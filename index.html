Welcome! To the coolest online gaming club. In our community we look forward to linking with gamers of all ages, creeds and genders to build a friendly and fun online gaming environment. We gather in parties and take on new adventures and challenges across different MMO games.

Games We Play

The world famous game, World of Warcraft A.K.A WoW, Prepare to enjoy endless hours of entertainment while going on fantastic quests, challenging battlegrounds and ruthless arenas with your club mates.

If shooting everything that moves is your poison then CS: GO is the game you want to play. Here in the Online Gaming Club you can party up in 2 teams to enjoy all the different maps available on this amazing first person shooter that never gets old.

Are you ready to take on the opposite team's nexus? Then think no more, League of Legends is here to quench that thirst for fast paced sandbox team strategy and fighting. Victory awaits you!

In this magical world, you'll gear up with your online club mates, to face some of the biggest and most challenging group battles as well as really fun quests that will lead you to great treasures.
